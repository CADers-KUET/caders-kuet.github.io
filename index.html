About CADers

Drawing is called the language of engineers. And it is needless to describe the importance of this language throughout an engineering carrier. As the demand of engineering rises across the globe, the whole designing procedure is converging towards computer aided systems. Thus various software industries are now providing their latest versions of software to pace with the manufacturing flow. As an engineer we should have clear concept about various functions of these software. To nourish an individual’s creativity and improve his/her CAD quality in these software, there should be a platform on which all the professionals and other engineering students can share their views and the club “CADers” is that platform. The full form of “CADers” is “Computer Aided Designers” and the motto of this club is “To promote engineering language among the engineering students and produce quality designers” This club is originated at Khulna University of Engineering and Technology (KUET). The main functionality of this club will be arranging seminars/workshop on different professional CAD software time to time, making communication with various Design Company and personals nationwide as well as international. The club will also help as a trouble shooter among the members on various design topics. So, let’s go design and share our co-ordinates.
